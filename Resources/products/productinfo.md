# Product & Equipment Reference

With the combination of the xTool M2, Apparel Printer, P2S, F1, WonderPress, and Omni Printer, you can build a business that covers nearly every major personalization and maker market. Here's a comprehensive breakdown.

## Equipment

| Product | What it does |
|---|---|
| **M2** | Blade cutting, pen drawing, foil transfer, laser engraving, laser cutting |
| **P2S** | Large CO₂ laser for cutting and engraving wood, acrylic, leather, glass, stone, paper, rubber, fabric, and more |
| **F1** | Ultra-fast fiber + diode laser for engraving metals, plastics, leather, jewelry, tumblers, and event personalization |
| **Apparel Printer** | DTF apparel printing for cotton, polyester, blends, denim, nylon, and more |
| **WonderPress** | Heat pressing, DTF transfer, sublimation, vacuum forming, craft oven, acrylic bending, mold making |
| **Omni Printer** | UV printing, UV DTF, DTG, DTF, textured printing on rigid and fabric surfaces depending on configuration |
